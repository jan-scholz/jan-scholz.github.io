DRAFT: How to Make Millions of Dollars with AI

How to Make Millions of Dollars
February 22, 2023 · 2 min · Jan Scholz

How do you make millions of dollars with AI? Don’t use AI!

If your experience is anywhere close the majority of data scientists then you might not have had a hand in building ChatGPT. Instead you might have had to wait weeks to get access to the right data. Maybe you had data but it was not enough, imbalanced, or ridden with artifacts. Maybe you had to work with an old company-provisioned laptop, maybe GitLab and the cloud were not available for security reasons. Maybe the question you were asked to solve was ill-defined or your business stakeholders couldn’t make up their mind. Maybe you didn’t have the right resources to make progress. Your team was too small or didn’t have the right composition. You didn’t have access to the right software and hardware. Maybe you couldn’t make progress because you had to spend all your time sitting in Scrum meetings and building reports for leadership.

If any of this sounds familiar you probably share these experiences with the plurality of data scientists. Most data scientists do not work on the most impactful questions nor do they get to use the best data to build the fanciest models. Instead, data scientists are often asked to quickly hack together some code that makes some useful predictions.

Common examples are

  • churn prediction
  • time series forecasts
  • recommendations

that solve a problem that a company has had for years and no human could ever come close to solving.
