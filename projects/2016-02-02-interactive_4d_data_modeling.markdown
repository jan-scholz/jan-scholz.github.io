Most MRI data viewers require switching to external programs to calculate statistical maps.
This shiny application fits and plots statistical models interactively while browsing the voxel data.
The ability to observe model modifications instantly greatly facilitates the model search and refinement process.
Additionally, trying out different models can often facilitate better understanding of the data.
